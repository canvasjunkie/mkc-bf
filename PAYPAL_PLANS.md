## PayPal Setup Notes

1. Go to PayPal Business → Products & Services → Subscriptions
2. Create a Product called "Bot Factory"
3. Add 2 Plans: Starter ($9/mo) and Pro ($29/mo)
4. Copy the Plan IDs for your code
5. Free tier = no PayPal plan, just user signup
